⚡ Off-Grid – Educational Game on Load Shedding Awareness
📌 Overview

Off-Grid is an interactive educational game designed to raise awareness about load shedding and its impact on communities, the economy, and daily life in South Africa.

The game leverages storytelling, simulation, and human-computer interaction (HCI) principles to create an engaging and informative experience.

🎯 Mission: Educate users on load shedding while promoting awareness of alternative energy solutions through interactive gameplay.

🚀 Features
🎮 Multi-Stage Gameplay
🧩 Stage 1: Interview (Quiz System)
Tests user's knowledge on load shedding
Multiple-choice questions
Progression-based system

💰 Stage 2: Coin Collector
Simulates financial decision-making
Players must raise funds for energy solutions:
Generator
Solar Panels
Wind Turbines
Introduces risk/reward mechanics (bombs vs rewards)

🕵️ Stage 3: Investigator
Problem-solving and exploration
Players recover stolen funds
Time-based challenge with increasing complexity

⚙️ Difficulty Levels
  Beginner
  Intermediate
  Expert

Each level dynamically adjusts:

-Time constraints
-Number of objectives
-Game complexity
-Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)

The game was designed with a strong focus on usability and engagement:

🎨 User-friendly interface (menu-driven & point-and-click)
🔊 Audio-visual feedback to enhance interaction
🖱️ Ergonomic design for intuitive navigation
🔁 Iterative prototyping based on user feedback
🌍 Real-World Problem Addressed
⚡ What is Load Shedding?

Load shedding is the controlled reduction of electricity supply to prevent total grid failure due to high demand.

📉 Impact:
Reduced productivity in businesses
Disruption of digital infrastructure
Increased operational costs
Safety and security risks
Interruption of daily life
💡 Proposed Solutions in the Game

Players explore real-world alternatives such as:

☀️ Solar Energy
🌬️ Wind Energy
🔋 Energy Storage Systems (Inverters/Batteries)

The game promotes:

Sustainable thinking
Community awareness
Better energy decision-making
